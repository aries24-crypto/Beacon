Privacy & Sandbox Guidelines

At Beacon, we believe absolute data privacy is a human right. All processed variables reside strictly on your current device sandbox.

  • Documents and profiles are managed directly using browser-level sandbox storage.
  • Your learning records are never permanently transmitted to central databases.
  • Clearing your web browser history instantly purges all temporary guest profiles and databases.

About Beacon

Beacon is a next-generation study platform engineered to extract semantic structure from academic documents. We draw UI inspiration from Notion, ChatGPT, Arc Browser, and Linear to maintain clean, premium workspace flows with responsive interfaces.

Built using modern Web APIs and fluid styling structures to keep operations swift on any device.
