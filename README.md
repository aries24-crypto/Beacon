# Beacon

> **Guiding You Through Every Lesson.**

Beacon is an AI-powered learning companion that helps students transform their study materials into meaningful understanding. Instead of simply generating answers, Beacon acts as a guide, helping learners navigate their notes, prepare for exams, and build lasting knowledge.

## 🌟 Inspiration

Students today are overwhelmed by PDFs, lecture slides, handwritten notes, and countless online resources. The hardest part isn't finding information, it's knowing **where to begin**.

Just as a beacon guides travelers through uncertainty, **Beacon** guides learners through their academic journey.

## 🚀 What Beacon Does

Upload your study material and let Beacon become your personal learning guide.

- 📄 AI-generated summaries
- 🧠 Flashcards for active recall
- ❓ Adaptive quizzes
- 📊 Smart reports & insights
- 📝 Revision notes
- 🎯 Personalized study guidance

## ✨ Key Features

### 📂 Upload Anything
Supports PDFs, notes, presentations, and other study materials.

### 🤖 AI Learning Companion
Beacon analyzes your content and explains concepts in a clear, structured way.

### 📖 Smart Summaries
Convert lengthy notes into concise, easy-to-read summaries.

### 🧠 Flashcards
Automatically generate flashcards to reinforce memory.

### 📝 Practice Quizzes
Create AI-generated quizzes based entirely on your uploaded material.

### 📈 Learning Insights
Identify weak areas and receive recommendations on what to revise next.
